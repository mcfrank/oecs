title: "About"
---

The Open Encyclopedia of Cognitive Science (OECS) is an online, open, and authoritative resource about the nature of minds—what they are, how they grow, how they interact, and how they differ from one another.

OECS is a freely-available, growing collection of peer-reviewed articles introducing key topics to a broad audience of students and scholars. It is published by the MIT Press and supported by the James S. McDonnell Foundation and the Allen Institute for AI.
